# Project Overview

Appointment scheduling SaaS for the Indonesian market — a Calendly alternative with WhatsApp, Google Calendar, Email integrations, and local payment gateway (future). Modular monolith with multi-tenancy and RBAC, targeting clinics, barbershops, consultants, and other service businesses.

## User Stories:
As a business owner, I want to manage my client appointments so that I can reduce no-shows. The client can book an appointment online and get a reminder via WhatsApp, also can cancel and reschedule.
